Am I Ready for Level 4 at ZiggyFeet?

Level 4 is for dancers who have built solid foundations and are ready to train to be better social dancers, with more intention, detail, and depth.

You don’t need to be perfect, you need to have put in the effort to establish the groundwork for further growth.

2. Timing & Technique Awareness

L3A Advanced Whips and Turns Concepts

Whip with single inside/outside turn,
Whip with double inside/outside turn,
Open whip,
Barrel roll,

L3B Movement and Expression
Able to use basic moves to set up and hit something in the music,
Understanding of basic musical structure,
Able to predict music,
Identify common musical genres,
Able to isolate different layers in a song,
Comfortable with using body isolations (shoulder rolls / shimmies, chest pops / rolls, hip movement, arm styling) in a social dance,
Inside turn duck,
Underarm inside turn duck

L3C Advanced Fundamentals
Partial full delay in triple steps,
Able to use sending / receiving foot,
Able to achieve constant and consistent flight,
Able to roll feet,
Swing action

L3D Lead and Follow Technique
Telemark,
Sugar push with leaders exit,
Accordion sugar push,
1 degree to create away intention

L3E Footwork & Styling
Able to do anchor variations (coaster/sailor step),
Variations of ___ ball changes - kick / press / tap / brush; in different directions - front / side / back,
Comfortable with ending pattern on the off-foot and how to recover,
Delayed 1,
Hitch

L3F Dancing to Fast/Slow/Blues Music
Comfortable and confident dancing to fast songs (120 bpm and above),
Comfortable and confident dancing to slow songs (80 bpm and below),
Comfortable and confident dancing to blues,
Able to apply swung rhythm

L3G Weight Support

Outside turn with lean,

Accelerated 1-2 (with delayed 1) into hip catch or whip,
Accelerated sugar push 1-2 into decelerated 3&4,
Decelerated outside turn,
Accelerated hip catch exit into decelerated anchor
