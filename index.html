Am I Ready for Level 4 at ZiggyFeet?

Level 4 is for dancers who have built solid foundations and are ready to train to be better social dancers, with more intention, detail, and depth.

You don’t need to be perfect, you need to have put in the effort to establish the groundwork for further growth.

Take a moment and reflect honestly:

1. Syllabus Confidence

2. Timing & Technique Awareness

3. Social Dance Experience

4. Mindset for Level 4

View Syllabus Guide

L1: Left Side Pass, Sugar Push, Inside Turn, Underarm Turn, Underarm inside turn, Sugar Tuck, Tuck Pass, Outside Turn, Close hold + Starter step, Underarm cut-off, footwork - double / triple step, double-triple-step-triple-step

L2A
Basic Whip, Basket Whip, Same-side Whip, Fake Whip, Reverse Whip, Turning Basic, Whip with Single/Double Inside Turn, Whip with Single/Double Outside Turn

L2B
Basic Handchanges (L to L, R to R, R to L, double handholds), Folds

L2C
Turn Technique, Turning Frame, Sugar Push Rock n Go, Inside Turn Rock n Go, Sugar Tuck Rock n Go, Outside Turn Rock n Go

L2D
Count Extensions, Staccato and Legato movements, Inside & Outside Turn Hip Catch, Slingshot, Invitations, Basic Musicality

L3 Concepts
Music and Expression, Advanced Fundamentals, Lead and Follow Technique, Footwork and Styling, Dancing to Fast and Slow Music, Weight Support, Accel & Decel

At Ziggyfeet, progression isn’t about rushing — it’s about building dancers who feel confident, capable, and proud of their foundations.

“In school, the worst students repeat levels. Dance is the opposite, it’s the best students who repeat levels.” - Wee Tze Yi
